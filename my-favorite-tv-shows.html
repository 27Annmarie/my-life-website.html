Life of Annmarie - Favorite TV Shows

A few of my favorite tv shows are:


Happy Gilmore

Happy Gilmore is about a man who lives on hockey. When he cannot fulfil his dream
someone comes to him and tells him he should play golf. Then the unexpected happens
because he does not want to lose his grandma's house.



Home Alone

Home Alone is about a boy and his family who take a family trip. However,
they forget one boy who stays home alone. They forget the boy but he still has
the time of his life.
